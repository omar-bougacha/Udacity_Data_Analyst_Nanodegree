Ford GoBike Data Analysis¶

by Omar BOUGACHA¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this presentation, I propose to compare some of the attributes of the Ford GoBike bike rides dataset. The aim is to present some of the insights of the data, compare the evolution of the performances, and give a support for future business decisions.

Dataset Overview¶

After wrangling, the dataset of the bike rides information from the Ford GoBike is composed of:

  • One table for bike rides for 2018 containing 1851950 entries with 8 features
  • One table for bike rides for 2019 containing 2262979 entries with 8 features (Without the data of December)
  • One table for stations' information for 2018 containing 331 entries with 4 features
  • One table for stations' information for 2019 containing 428 entries with 4 features

Analysis Overview¶

During this analysis, I propose to study the users' behaviour. Therefore, I investigate the following questions:

  • How the duration of the bike ride are distributed?
  • During which period(s) of the day there are more bike rides?
  • What day(s) of the week present more bike rides?
  • During which month(s) have the more dense bike usage?
  • How is the flux of bike rides on some stations?
  • How did all these performances evolve between 2018 and 2019?

We can note that the distribution of the bike rides over the hours of the day are bimodal for both years. The distribution has two peaks: one around 8 and the other around 17. These values correspond to the working hours start and end. However, we can think of solutions to improve the bike usage during the other hours of the day. Maybe Propose some lower costs.
